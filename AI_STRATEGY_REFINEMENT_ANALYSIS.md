### Risks of AI-Heavy Positioning

2. **Technical complexity:** May scare non-technical buyers  
3. **Moving target:** AI landscape changes rapidly

### Mitigation Strategies
1. **Focus on practical benefits:** "Better job discovery" not "AI magic"
2. **Simple implementation:** "One line of code" remains key message
3. **Future-proof positioning:** "Built for whatever comes next"
