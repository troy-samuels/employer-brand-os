## INTERVIEW PROTOCOL FRAMEWORK

### Pre-Interview Preparation (10 minutes per interview)

**Company Research Checklist:**
- [ ] Company size, industry, location count
- [ ] Current job postings analysis (career page review)
- [ ] ATS system identification (job posting source analysis)  
- [ ] Recent hiring news or challenges (Google alerts, LinkedIn posts)
- [ ] Compliance requirements (state/local law research)

**Personalization Elements:**
- Specific compliment about company/role
- Reference to recent company news or achievements  
- Tailored questions based on industry/size
- Relevant pain points based on company profile

### Interview Structure (45 minutes)

#### Opening (5 minutes)
- Personal introduction and research context
- Interview recording permission and confidentiality assurance
- Expected interview length and structure overview
- Initial rapport building and comfort establishment

#### Current State Assessment (15 minutes)

**Job Posting Process:**
- "Walk me through how you post a job opening today."
- "What tools and systems are involved in that process?"
- "How many people are typically involved in getting a job posted?"
- "What's the timeline from job req to public posting?"

**Compliance Management:**
- "How do you ensure pay transparency compliance across your locations?"
- "What's your process for keeping up with changing employment laws?"
- "Have you had any compliance concerns or issues recently?"
- "How much time does compliance management take monthly?"

**Performance Measurement:**
- "How do you track job posting performance?"
- "What metrics matter most for your job postings?"
- "Have you noticed changes in how candidates discover your jobs?"
- "Do you track traffic sources to your career page?"

#### Pain Point Discovery (10 minutes)

**Current Challenges:**
- "What's the most frustrating part of your current job posting process?"
- "If you could wave a magic wand, what would you fix first?"
- "What keeps you up at night regarding hiring or compliance?"
- "Where do you spend the most time on tasks that feel inefficient?"

**Impact Quantification:**
- "How much time does your team spend on job posting management weekly?"
- "What's the cost impact of compliance management?"
- "Have you missed out on candidates due to visibility issues?"
- "What would happen if you had a compliance violation?"

#### Solution Validation (15 minutes)

**BrandOS Concept Introduction:**
- "I'd like to share a concept and get your reaction..."
- [Present BrandOS Smart Pixel concept with visual]
- "What's your initial reaction to this approach?"
- "Would this solve problems you're currently facing?"

**Implementation Feasibility:**
- "How would you evaluate a tool like this?"
- "What would need to be true for you to consider implementing it?"
- "Who else would be involved in this decision?"
- "What concerns would you have about implementation?"

**Pricing Sensitivity:**
- "How much do you currently spend on HR technology monthly?"
- "What would be a reasonable price point for this solution?"
- "How do you typically budget for compliance tools?"
- "Would you pay more for automated vs manual compliance?"

#### Closing and Next Steps (5 minutes)

**Additional Insights:**
- "What haven't I asked that I should have?"
- "Who else should I talk to about this topic?"
- "What resources do you use to stay informed about HR trends?"
- "Any final thoughts or advice?"

**Follow-up Commitment:**
- Research report delivery timeline
- Free compliance audit offer
- Early access notification process
- Thank you and contact information

### Post-Interview Analysis (15 minutes per interview)

#### Immediate Capture:
- [ ] Pain point intensity scoring (1-10 scale)
- [ ] Solution fit assessment (1-10 scale)  
- [ ] Buying authority confirmation (Yes/No)
- [ ] Price sensitivity range ($X-Y per month)
- [ ] Implementation barriers identification
- [ ] Referral opportunities notation

#### Synthesis Questions:
1. **What surprised you most about this conversation?**
2. **How does this person's challenges compare to previous interviews?**
3. **What patterns are emerging across similar customer types?**
4. **What assumptions were validated or invalidated?**
5. **What questions should be added/modified for future interviews?**

#### Action Items:
- [ ] Follow-up commitments scheduled
- [ ] Referral introductions requested
- [ ] Additional research needs identified
- [ ] Product/service adjustments noted
